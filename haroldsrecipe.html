How to make Chicken Alfredo.

List of Ingredients

  • 1 lb of boneless chicken breast
  • 2 cans of Alfredo sauce in a can
  • 1 or 2 boxes of fettuccine pasta
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Garlic powder seasoning

Steps for preparation

    1. Pour 1/2 a cup of water into a pot. Bring to the boil.
    2. Cut the boneless chicken into quarters.
    3. Cut 1 clove of garlic into tiny pieces.
    4. Season your chicken with garlic powder, black pepper, and salt.
    5. Place the seasoned chicken into the skillet and stir.
    6. Place a cover or lid over the skillet.

    Steps for cooking

    1. Take the noodles out of the pot.
    2. Pour 1 1/2 cans of Alfredo sauce into the skillet with the chicken.
    3. Stir the ingredients and cover with the lid.
    4. Place the noodles in the skillet with the chicken and Alfredo sauce.
    5. Stir and cover with lid.
    6. Place the pasta on a nice dish and serve it.
